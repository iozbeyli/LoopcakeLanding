Learning Management with Version Control

Everything you need for programming education.


Software engineering is a growing part of current era. Educating qualified people for industry and academy is essential for our future. Loopcake lets people in universities to focus on education, and ease unnecessary struggles by creating perfect environment for prospective computer engineers.

What We Do

We provide a modern, and user friendly learning management system for courses. Furthermore, with our integrated Git service, students will be able to benefit from advantages of version control systems.

Other VCS solution addresses wide variety of subject, from big enterprises to hobbyists. As a result, they become difficult for beginners, and most of the time students and instructors avoid using VCSs in their course projects. Hence as studies show, most of the graduates do not have enough knowledge about version control.

By using Loopcake, not only students will gradually learn version control by experience, but also, instructors will be able to track progresses of their student groups easily.

  • Project Management Tool

    Make it easier to distribute group workload, and track student effort and progress

  • Plagiarism Tool

    Catch cheating attempts

  • Online Code Editor

    Access and work on programming projects anywhere

  • Gamification

    Encourage student effort by giving badges and establishing a friendly competition among students

  • Data Loss Prevention

    Do not lose any data, and easily express gained knowledge throughout academic life

  • Student Data Visualization

    Gain insight into students’ development and see the results of education in a comprehensive way

  • User Friendly Git Client

    Start to use version control at the first year of your education, and gradually learn more
